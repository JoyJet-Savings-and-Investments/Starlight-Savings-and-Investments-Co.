About Us

Starlight Investments LLC

Starlight Investments LLC is a premier North American real estate investment and asset management platform operating a public Telegram channel at @StarLightInvestmentsLLC. The platform provides investors with curated opportunities across the United States and Canada, focusing on multifamily residential assets.

The firm employs four distinct investment strategies: Core, Core Plus, Value Add, and Development — each designed to match specific risk-return profiles across market cycles.

With over 29 years of experience, Starlight has grown into a global real estate investor achieving success throughout market cycles and risk spectrums. U.S. residential residences are managed by industry-leading property management companies, ensuring exceptional resident experiences through regular satisfaction surveys and dedicated resident portals.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about investing with Starlight Investments.

What is the minimum investment amount?
Our minimum investment starts at $10,000 for the Core Plus Lite plan. Other plans range from $25,000 to $150,000 depending on the strategy and class. We recommend reviewing the Investment Plans section for full details.
How do I receive returns on my investment?
Returns are distributed according to your plan's dividend frequency — monthly, quarterly, semi-annual, or at the back-end upon exit. All distributions are processed securely via Stripe to your linked bank account or card.
Can I withdraw my investment early?
Early withdrawal options vary by plan. Short-term plans (1-3 years) offer more flexibility, while long-term plans are designed for committed capital. Please contact us at starlightinvestmentsmanagement@gmail.com for specific terms.
Is my investment secured by real assets?
Yes. All investments are backed by physical residential real estate assets across Canada and the United States. Each property is managed by industry-leading property management companies with regular resident satisfaction surveys.
How do I get started?
Simply click the "Invest Now" button on any plan, complete the secure Stripe payment form, and you'll receive immediate confirmation. Our team will follow up within 24 hours with your investment documentation and portal access.
How do I contact support?
You can reach us directly via email at starlightinvestmentsmanagement@gmail.com or through our Telegram channel at t.me/StarLightInvestmentsLLC. We typically respond within 2-4 hours during business hours.
